In my second year of undergraduate studies, I applied to the FOCAL (Free and Open-source Creative Art Library) fellowship as part of the FOSSEE (Free/Libre and Open Source Software for Education) Project at the Indian Institue of Technology, Bombay. Applicants were asked to submit seven tasks and my favourite is my submission for the object image manipulation task.

This is my visualisation of global warming as the Earth being fried like an egg. Made in the GNU Image Manipulation Program using public-domain photos.

I was not selected for the FOCAL fellowship, but FOSSEE did have a requirement in their Health and Nutrition project. As a trial, I contributed this illustration for an informative deck on child nutrition. This was my first experience with vector illustration. Made in Inkscape.
