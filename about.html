My name is Urvi Ashturkar.

There are blind spots in introspection, so I decided to research myself. I have been asking three questions to people who have worked with me or who know me well.

1. What am I doing or talking about when I seem most energized and happy?

appreciating beauty

brainstorming gift ideas

making for others

talking about obscure facts

2. When I seem my best, the exact thing I am doing is -

visualising in 3D

problem solving

researching in depth

designing meaningful/witty visuals

3. What are your three favorite qualities you see in me? (Do your best to use one word per quality.)

detail-oriented

determined

organised

openly communicative

attentive learner

This is a work in progress; nevertheless the responses so far are insightful. The questions have been taken from this article from 80000hours.org.
